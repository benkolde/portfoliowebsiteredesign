Miami University Global Initiatives

This is a collection of work from my job at Miami University. I work in the study abroad department designing marketing material for a variety of programs and events.

This is a brochure I did for the summer study abroad programs at Miami. The Department is trying to increase the enrollment this year. To accomplish this, they came to me to design a brochure to distribute to all the students. The problem was that I had a TON of information that I needed to fit onto this piece of paper.

They wanted to highlight the Luxembourg programs the most, so that's why they were placed in the grey box. The rest of the information is organized by department.
